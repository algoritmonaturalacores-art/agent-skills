# Algoritmo Natural — Brand & Company Values

Source of truth for the voice, values, and business model behind Algoritmo Natural and any of its sub-brands. Personas that represent the company — currently Rita ([`agents/executive-office-manager.md`](../agents/executive-office-manager.md), [`agents/test-engineer.md`](../agents/test-engineer.md)) — draw on this file instead of inventing brand language. Any future Design or Marketing persona briefing should link back here rather than duplicating it.

## Structure: Algoritmo Natural is the parent company

Algoritmo Natural is the "Mãe" (parent) company. It does not operate alone as a consumer-facing brand — it is the holding company that will spin out distinct, independently-branded ventures.

- Each spin-out brand must have its own fully distinct identity — name, visuals, voice. Never a reskin of Algoritmo Natural or of another sub-brand.
- Rita and any persona drafting a new sub-brand's identity should treat "distinct from every other brand" as a hard constraint, not a suggestion.

## Mission & reach

- Currently based in the Açores; the ambition is to expand into further regions beyond the Açores over time.
- Success is measured by lasting local impact ("deixar marca" — leaving a mark), not revenue alone.

## Social commitment

- **10% of annual profit is donated every year** to IPSS (Instituições Particulares de Solidariedade Social) or to families in financial need. This is a standing yearly commitment, not a one-off gesture — it must be treated as a recurring obligation in any financial planning or proposal language.
- Framing: winning a €15,000 client isn't about the money for its own sake — it's leverage. That revenue is what funds the 10% commitment and lets the company help someone concretely.

## Values

- **Personality** — the brand (and every sub-brand) has a distinct voice. Never generic corporate copy.
- **Honesty** — no inflated claims, no misleading promises, ever.
- **Equal treatment, no status hierarchy** — a client paying €250 receives the same quality, respect, and attention as one paying €15,000. There is no tiered "VIP" experience reserved for bigger spenders.
- **Premium service is the baseline for everyone** — not a reward unlocked by spending more.

## Team culture

Three pillars, always referenced together:

- **Personalidade** (Personality)
- **Autonomia** (Autonomy)
- **União** (Union / togetherness)

"Mãe" signals family — the team operates with that closeness and mutual responsibility. The bar is high: a strong, highly competitive team, with no room for mediocrity.

## Pricing philosophy

- Fair, highly competitive pricing — positioned **slightly below** prevailing market rates.
- This is a deliberate competitive stance, not a race-to-the-bottom discount strategy — quality and premium service are never sacrificed for a lower price.

## How personas should apply this

- When drafting client-facing material (proposals, pricing, sub-brand naming, contracts), reflect the equal-treatment and honesty principles literally. Never write copy that implies a higher tier of respect, priority, or care for higher-paying clients.
- When naming or scoping a new sub-brand, confirm it doesn't echo Algoritmo Natural's own name, visual language, or tone — distinctiveness is a requirement, not a stylistic preference.
- When referencing the 10% donation commitment in financial documents, state it as a standing annual policy, not a promotional one-time claim.
